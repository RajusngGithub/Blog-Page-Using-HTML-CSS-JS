Places Where Bloggers visit the most

Jog Falls

Jog Falls, located in Sivamogga Karnataka, India, is one of the country's highest waterfalls. Plunging from a height of 830 feet, the falls offer a breathtaking view, especially during the monsoon season. It's a must-visit for nature lovers.Surrounded by lush greenery, Jog Falls is created by the Sharavathi River and consists of four distinct cascades: Raja, Rani, Rover, and Rocket.

Aagumbe

Aagumbe, located in Shivamogga Karnataka, India, is often called the "Cherrapunji of the South" due to its high rainfall. Nestled in the Western Ghats, it boasts lush rainforests, stunning sunsets, and diverse wildlife, including the elusive King Cobra. Aagumbe is renowned for its trekking trails, serene landscapes, and as a significant site for rainforest research and conservation.

Kodaikanal

Kodaikanal, nestled in the Tamil Nadu hills, is a popular hill station known for its scenic beauty and pleasant climate. Often called the "Princess of Hill Stations," it features misty hills, dense forests, and serene lakes. Key attractions include Kodai Lake, Coaker's Walk, and Bryant Park. Ideal for nature lovers and trekkers, Kodaikanal offers a tranquil escape from city life.
